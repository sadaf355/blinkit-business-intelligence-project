# 📘 Dashboard Guide

## Overview

The Blinkit Business Intelligence Dashboard is an interactive reporting solution developed using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance across Sales, Customers, Marketing, Operations, and Inventory.

The report is designed to help stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), analyze trends, evaluate operational efficiency, and make informed business decisions.

---

# Dashboard Navigation

The report consists of four interactive pages.

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Executive Overview | High-level business performance summary |
| Sales & Product Intelligence | Product performance and sales analysis |
| Customer & Marketing Intelligence | Customer satisfaction and marketing performance |
| Operations & Supply Chain Intelligence | Delivery and inventory performance |

Users can navigate between pages using the navigation panel located on the left side of the dashboard.

---

# Global Filters

The following filters are available across the report.

## Date Filter

Allows users to analyze business performance for a selected date range.

Business Use:

- Monthly analysis
- Quarterly analysis
- Yearly analysis

---

## Customer Segment Filter

Allows users to filter dashboard metrics based on customer segments.

Examples:

- Premium Customers
- Regular Customers
- New Customers
- Inactive Customers

Business Use:

- Segment-wise sales analysis
- Customer behaviour comparison
- Marketing targeting
