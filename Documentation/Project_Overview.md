\# 📌 Project Overview



\## Introduction



The Blinkit Business Intelligence Project is an end-to-end analytics solution developed to simulate a real-world business intelligence workflow for an online grocery delivery platform. The project demonstrates how raw transactional data can be transformed into meaningful business insights using modern data analytics tools and techniques.



The solution integrates Microsoft Excel for data cleaning, PostgreSQL for database management, SQL for business analysis, and Power BI for interactive dashboard development. The final output is a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor overall business performance, identify trends, evaluate marketing effectiveness, analyze customer behavior, and improve operational efficiency.



This project follows the complete Business Intelligence lifecycle, from raw data preparation to executive-level reporting.



\---



\# Business Problem



Online grocery delivery companies generate large volumes of transactional and operational data every day. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult for decision-makers to identify growth opportunities, monitor business performance, optimize inventory, and improve customer satisfaction.



The primary challenge is converting raw business data into actionable insights that support strategic and operational decision-making.



This project addresses that challenge by developing an interactive Business Intelligence solution that enables stakeholders to:



\- Monitor key business metrics in real time.

\- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.

\- Evaluate product performance and profitability.

\- Measure marketing campaign effectiveness.

\- Track delivery efficiency.

\- Monitor inventory health.

\- Improve overall business performance through data-driven insights.



\---



\# Project Objectives



The main objectives of this project are:



\- Clean and preprocess raw business data.

\- Design a structured relational database.

\- Perform SQL-based business analysis.

\- Develop meaningful KPIs using DAX.

\- Build interactive Power BI dashboards.

\- Generate business insights for decision-making.

\- Demonstrate an end-to-end Business Intelligence workflow.



\---



\# Dataset Overview



The project consists of eight interconnected datasets representing different business functions.



| Table | Description |

|--------|-------------|

| Customers | Customer information and segmentation |

| Products | Product details including category and margin |

| Orders | Order-level transaction information |

| Order Items | Product-level details for each order |

| Delivery Performance | Delivery time and status information |

| Customer Feedback | Ratings and customer sentiment |

| Marketing Performance | Campaign performance metrics |

| Inventory | Inventory movement and stock information |



These datasets were imported into PostgreSQL, analyzed using SQL, and connected in Power BI through a relational data model.
